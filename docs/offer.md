# ArgusReach — Offer Definition v1

## The One-Line Pitch
ArgusReach gives small and mid-sized businesses a fully automated AI sales development system that finds qualified leads, sends personalized outreach, handles follow-up, and books meetings — without hiring an SDR.

## The Problem We Solve
SMBs in Tampa Bay / Florida can't afford a full-time SDR ($60–80K/year salary). They don't have time to do outbound themselves. Generic email blasts don't work. AI tools like Apollo and Instantly exist but require technical setup they don't have time for. The result: no consistent pipeline, relying entirely on referrals and word of mouth.

## What ArgusReach Does (The Service)
Argus is a done-for-you AI sales outreach system. Every month:
1. **Finds leads** — builds a targeted list of ideal prospects using verified data sources
2. **Writes personalized outreach** — AI-generated emails that reference specific details about each prospect (not mail merge garbage)
3. **Runs multi-touch sequences** — 3–5 email follow-ups spaced over 2–3 weeks
4. **Qualifies responses** — filters interested replies, handles basic objections
5. **Books meetings** — positive responses get routed to a booking link or handed directly to the client
6. **Reports monthly** — leads contacted, open rates, reply rates, meetings booked

## What We Are NOT
- Not a SaaS tool they log into (no self-serve yet)
- Not running ads
- Not SEO or content marketing
- Not cold calling (yet)
- Not giving financial or legal advice

## Target Customer (ICP v1)
**Primary vertical: Financial Services (Tampa Bay)**
- Independent RIAs and wealth management firms (2–20 employees)
- Insurance agencies (independent, not captive)
- Mortgage brokers and loan officers
- CFP/financial planning practices

**Why financial services first:**
- High LTV clients (they close one client = $5–50K+ in revenue)
- Willing to pay for qualified meetings
- Vito speaks their language — instant credibility
- Underserved by generic AI tools (compliance concerns scare others away)

**Secondary vertical (Month 2–3): Professional services**
- Commercial real estate brokers
- Business attorneys
- Staffing/recruiting firms
- B2B service companies (IT, accounting, consulting)
