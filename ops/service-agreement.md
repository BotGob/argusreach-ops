## 1. Services

Provider agrees to perform the following services on behalf of Client:

a) Research and build a targeted list of prospective contacts ("Prospects") matching Client's defined Ideal Customer Profile ("ICP"), as established during onboarding.

b) Write personalized outreach email sequences on Client's behalf, to be sent from Client's designated email address or a Provider-configured sending address.

c) Execute and manage automated email sequences to Prospects, including follow-up touches as defined by the selected service plan.

d) Monitor inbound replies to outreach emails and route positive or interested responses to Client in a timely manner.

e) Provide a monthly activity summary including contacts reached, replies received, and meetings booked.

All outreach is conducted in Client's name. Provider will not identify itself as the author or operator of any outreach communications. Client acknowledges that all outreach is subject to Client's review and approval prior to deployment.

## 2. Plan & Pricing

**Selected Plan:** [Starter / Growth / Scale]
**Monthly Retainer:** $[750 / 1,500 / 2,500] per month
**One-Time Setup Fee:** $500 (due upon signing — covers onboarding, ICP research, list build, sequence writing, and all systems setup)
**Minimum Engagement:** 3 months from the date of first campaign launch

Monthly retainer billing begins on the date of first campaign launch (when first outreach emails go out), not the date of signing. The setup fee covers all work performed prior to launch. Monthly retainer is due on the 1st of each active month following campaign launch. Invoices unpaid after 10 days may result in campaign pause.

After the initial 3-month period, this Agreement continues month-to-month until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

## 3. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

a) Provide accurate and complete information during onboarding, including ICP details, sending identity, and any do-not-contact lists.

b) Review and approve all email sequences before campaign launch.

c) Respond to hot lead notifications within 1 business day to avoid prospect cooling.

d) Ensure the designated sending email address is active and accessible.

e) Comply with all applicable laws governing commercial email outreach, including CAN-SPAM and any state-specific regulations.

f) If providing a client-supplied prospect list, warrant that all contacts on that list have a legitimate business basis for outreach and that Client has not purchased, scraped, or otherwise obtained the list in violation of applicable law. Client assumes full legal responsibility for any client-supplied contact data.

g) Notify Provider of any compliance constraints, regulatory requirements, or restrictions applicable to their industry before campaign launch.

## 4. Compliance & Regulatory

Client represents that they have reviewed all outreach materials prior to deployment and that the content complies with applicable regulations governing their industry, including but not limited to SEC, FINRA, state insurance department, and HIPAA requirements where applicable.

Provider does not provide legal or compliance advice. Client is solely responsible for ensuring outreach content meets applicable regulatory standards.

All outreach includes an unsubscribe mechanism. Provider will immediately remove any contact who requests removal from future communications.

## 5. Performance & Expectations

Provider does not guarantee specific results including but not limited to: open rates, reply rates, meetings booked, or revenue generated. All projected benchmarks provided are estimates based on industry averages and are not contractual commitments.

Provider commits to: accurate contact sourcing, professional-quality email copy, consistent campaign execution, transparent reporting, and prompt notification of any deliverability issues.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep confidential all non-public information received from the other party in connection with this Agreement, including but not limited to Client's prospect lists, ICP details, business strategy, and client data. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

Provider will not sell Client's prospect or client data, share it with other clients, or use it for any purpose outside of performing the Services described herein. Client acknowledges that Provider uses vetted third-party platforms (including but not limited to CRM and email delivery tools) as subprocessors in the ordinary course of delivering the Services. Such platforms are bound by their own privacy and data protection policies. Provider will not authorize any subprocessor to use Client data for purposes beyond service delivery.

Upon termination, Provider will deliver all campaign data, prospect lists, and email assets to Client within 5 business days.
