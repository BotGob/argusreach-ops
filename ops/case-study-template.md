## How to Get This Quote from Your Client

Email to send after a good result:

```
Hi [First Name],

Really pleased with the results from last month — [X] meetings booked is a great outcome.

I'm putting together a brief case study to share with other firms we're talking to. Would you be willing to be featured? I'd keep it focused on the results, not your specific client details. I'd also share a draft with you before publishing anything.

And if you have a sentence or two about your experience, that would be incredibly helpful. Something like:
- What the situation was before
- What changed after working with us
- What you'd tell another advisor considering it

No obligation — but if you're open to it, it would mean a lot.

Vito
```

## How to Use the Case Study

1. **PDF version:** Copy into Google Doc → export as PDF → attach to proposal emails
2. **On website:** Create argusreach.com/results/[client-slug] (internal — share link, not indexed)
3. **In sales calls:** "Let me tell you about a similar firm we worked with..."
4. **LinkedIn post:** Summary version with client permission
5. **In the one-pager:** Replace "Projected Results" section with real numbers once available
