# ArgusReach — Client Service Agreement

**Effective Date:** [DATE]
**Client:** [CLIENT COMPANY NAME] ("Client")
**Provider:** ArgusReach LLC ("ArgusReach")

---

## 1. Services

ArgusReach provides B2B outbound email marketing services. Each month, ArgusReach will:

- Source a targeted list of prospects matching the Client's defined ideal customer profile (ICP) using third-party data platforms
- Create and manage an email outreach campaign on behalf of the Client
- Monitor inbound replies, classify each response, and draft reply emails for Client review
- Alert the Client via the ArgusReach admin portal and Telegram when a prospect reply requires attention
- Provide a monthly summary report including campaign activity and reply breakdown

The specific volume of prospects and monthly fee are defined in the selected plan (Exhibit A).

---

## 2. What ArgusReach Does Not Guarantee

Email outreach is an activity, not a guaranteed outcome. ArgusReach does not guarantee:

- A specific number of replies, meetings, or new clients
- Deliverability rates or inbox placement (affected by factors outside our control, including recipient mail servers and domain reputation)
- Revenue or business results of any kind

ArgusReach will perform the services described above with reasonable care and in accordance with industry best practices.

---

## 3. Client Responsibilities

For ArgusReach to perform the services effectively, the Client agrees to:

- Complete the onboarding intake form accurately and in full
- Review outreach campaigns in the Instantly platform before activating them (ArgusReach creates campaigns as drafts — Client activates)
- Review and respond to reply drafts in the admin portal within a reasonable timeframe
- Maintain the sending email account associated with their campaign (warm-up status, inbox access)
- Notify ArgusReach promptly of any changes to their ICP, target geography, or messaging

---

## 4. Compliance

Both parties agree to comply with applicable anti-spam laws, including the CAN-SPAM Act. ArgusReach will:

- Honor all unsubscribe requests immediately and permanently
- Maintain a global do-not-contact list across all Client campaigns
- Only contact prospects via professional business email addresses sourced from legitimate B2B data platforms

Client is solely responsible for ensuring that their use of ArgusReach services complies with any industry-specific regulations applicable to their business (including, but not limited to, HIPAA for healthcare organizations).

---

## 5. Fees and Payment

**Setup Fee:** A one-time setup fee is due at contract signing. Campaigns will not be launched until the setup fee is received.

**Monthly Retainer:** Billed monthly in advance. The monthly fee is due on the first of each month (or the anniversary of the contract start date). Services will be paused for accounts 10 or more days past due.

All fees are non-refundable once a campaign has been launched for that billing period.

Fees are outlined in Exhibit A.

---

## 6. Term and Cancellation

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis.

Either party may cancel with **30 days written notice**. Notice may be sent by email to the addresses on file.

Upon cancellation:
- ArgusReach will pause all active campaigns and cease outreach
- All prospect data sourced for the Client's campaigns will be made available for export for 30 days
- No refund will be issued for the current billing period

---

## 7. Data and Ownership

- **Client Data:** Any information provided by the Client (company details, ICP, messaging, contact lists) remains the property of the Client.
- **ArgusReach Systems:** The platform, code, automation workflows, AI prompts, and tooling used to deliver services are the exclusive property of ArgusReach.
- **Sourced Prospects:** Lead data sourced by ArgusReach on behalf of the Client is sourced from third-party B2B data providers. ArgusReach will provide the Client with a copy of each prospect list upon request.

---

## 8. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep the other's confidential business information private and not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent. This includes pricing, client lists, system details, and campaign strategy.

---

## 9. Limitation of Liability

ArgusReach's total liability to the Client for any claim arising out of this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the 30 days prior to the claim. ArgusReach is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages of any kind.
