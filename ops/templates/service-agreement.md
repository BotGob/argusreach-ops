**OUTBOUND PROSPECTING SERVICES AGREEMENT**

This Outbound Prospecting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[DATE]** by and between:

**ArgusReach** ("Provider"), a Florida limited liability company, and

**[CLIENT FIRM NAME]** ("Client"), located at **[CLIENT ADDRESS]**.

---

## 1. Services

Provider will deliver the following on behalf of Client each month:

- Research and build a targeted list of prospective contacts based on Client's specified ideal customer profile (ICP)
- Write and maintain a personalized outreach email sequence in Client's name and voice
- Send outreach emails from a dedicated email address owned and controlled by Client
- Classify and handle replies from prospects on Client's behalf
- Route interested prospects to Client's booking calendar
- Provide a monthly performance report covering contacts reached, replies received, and meetings booked

Client retains full control and approval rights over all outreach copy prior to launch. No emails are sent without Client's prior sequence approval.

---

## 2. Term and Renewal

This Agreement begins on the date of Client's first subscription payment and continues on a month-to-month basis. Either party may terminate with **30 days' written notice**. The initial term is **3 months** from the campaign launch date, after which the agreement converts to month-to-month.

---

## 3. Fees

**Setup Fee:** $500 (one-time, non-refundable). Due before campaign setup begins.

**Monthly Subscription:** Per the plan selected at intake:

| Plan | Monthly Fee | Prospects/Month |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $750 | 200 |
| Growth | $1,500 | 500 |
| Scale | $2,500 | 1,000 |

Monthly fees are billed in advance. Campaigns are not launched until payment is confirmed.

**Late Payments:** Accounts more than 7 days past due may be paused until payment is received.

---

## 4. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

- Provide accurate information about their business, target audience, and do-not-contact list in the intake form
- Set up a dedicated outreach email account (Google Workspace or Microsoft 365) and provide Provider with the email address and app password
- Work with their IT contact to add DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) provided by Provider within 5 business days of receipt
- Review and approve the outreach sequence within 5 business days of receiving the draft
- Connect their calendar to the Calendly booking page set up by Provider
- Promptly notify Provider of any prospect who should be added to the do-not-contact list
- Confirm meeting bookings through the Provider's portal when requested

---

## 5. Intellectual Property and Data

All prospect lists, sequences, and campaign materials built by Provider become the property of Client upon final payment of all amounts due. Client retains ownership of their outreach email address and domain.

Provider does not sell, share, or disclose Client data or prospect lists to any third party.

---

## 6. Compliance

Client agrees that all outreach conducted under this Agreement will comply with applicable law, including the CAN-SPAM Act and, where applicable, GDPR and CCPA. Client represents that they have a legitimate business purpose for contacting the prospects identified in their ICP.

Provider will honor all unsubscribe requests immediately and maintain a global do-not-contact list.

---

## 7. No Guarantee of Results

Provider will make every reasonable effort to reach qualified prospects and generate meeting opportunities. However, Provider makes no guarantee of specific outcomes, including meeting volume, conversion rates, or revenue generated. Results depend on factors including market conditions, Client's ICP, and the quality of Client's offer.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

Provider's liability under this Agreement is limited to the fees paid by Client in the prior 30 days. Provider is not liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages of any kind.

---

## 9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the terms of this Agreement and any proprietary information shared between them confidential. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

---

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice to the other party. Upon termination:

- Provider will stop all outreach immediately upon the effective termination date
- Client retains all prospect data and campaign materials
- No partial-month refunds are provided

Provider may terminate immediately (without notice) for non-payment, violation of this Agreement, or illegal use of the services.
