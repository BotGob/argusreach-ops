# ArgusReach — LinkedIn Outreach Script
## Vito finding ArgusReach's first clients

Use this TODAY. LinkedIn DMs are warm, free, and work fast for your network.

---

## THE STRATEGY

LinkedIn DMs work differently than cold email:
- Much shorter — people skim on mobile
- No subject line — first sentence IS the hook
- Connection = social proof — they already know who you are
- Response rate on 1st-degree connections: 15–30%

Do NOT pitch ArgusReach directly. Lead with curiosity about their pipeline problem.

---

## TIER 1: Warm (people you actually know)

**Message A — Former colleagues / industry contacts:**
```
Hey [Name], hope things are going well at [Firm].

Quick question — how are you sourcing new clients these days? Still mostly referrals?

Working on something in that space and figured you'd have a good perspective.
```

*Wait for response. If they engage, introduce ArgusReach naturally.*

**Message B — People you've met once:**
```
[Name] — great connecting at [event/context].

Random question: is proactive prospecting something [Firm] does at all, or is it all referral-driven?

Building something relevant and curious what the landscape looks like from where you sit.
```

**Message C — Closer network (can be more direct):**
```
[Name] — I'm building an AI outreach system for financial advisors / [their vertical]. Finds the right prospects, writes personalized emails as you, books meetings on your calendar. Fully done-for-you.

Testing it with a few founding clients at a reduced rate. Thought of you immediately.

Open to a 15-minute call to see if it makes sense for [Firm]?
```

---

## TIER 2: Cold (2nd degree or profile-matched)

**Connection request note (300 char limit):**
```
Hi [Name] — I work with [RIA / insurance / PT] firms in Tampa Bay building proactive pipelines outside of referrals. Done-for-you AI outreach — meetings booked on your calendar. Thought it was worth connecting.
```

**After they accept, wait 24 hours then:**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name].

Quick question — is [Firm] doing any proactive prospecting right now, or mostly referral-dependent?

I run ArgusReach, and we help [vertical] firms build a consistent outbound pipeline without hiring. Happy to share more if it's relevant.
```

---

## TIER 3: Inbound / Profile Viewers

If someone views your profile after you connect or post:
```
[Name] — noticed you viewed my profile. Happy coincidence — I work with [their apparent vertical] firms on building proactive pipelines.

Any chance outreach / new client acquisition is something you're thinking about?
```

---

## FOLLOW-UP (if no response after 5 days)

```
[Name] — just following up on my note. No worries if the timing's off.

If building a proactive pipeline outside of referrals is ever relevant — happy to connect. Feel free to reach out anytime.
```

Only one follow-up. Never more.

---

## WHEN THEY RESPOND WITH INTEREST

Don't over-explain in DM. Move to a call fast:

```
Great — the easiest thing is a quick 15-minute call. I can show you exactly what it looks like for [their specific vertical].

Here's my calendar: https://calendly.com/vito-argusreach/30min

What works for you this week?
```

If they want to know more before booking:
```
Happy to share more. In one sentence: we find your ideal prospects, write personalized emails as you, handle all follow-up, and route interested replies to your calendar. You just show up to the meetings.

More detail here: argusreach.com

Worth 15 min?
```

## WHO TO TARGET ON LINKEDIN (search criteria)

**Search 1 — RIA owners in Tampa Bay:**
- Title: "Financial Advisor" OR "Managing Partner" OR "Founder" OR "Owner"
- Location: Tampa Bay Area
- Industry: Financial Services
- Company size: 1–50

**Search 2 — Insurance agency principals:**
- Title: "Agency Owner" OR "Principal" OR "President"
- Location: Tampa Bay Area
- Industry: Insurance

**Search 3 — Your actual network first:**
- Go to My Network → Connections → Filter by industry: Financial Services
- Message everyone you know in the space before going to cold outreach

---

## DAILY ROUTINE (20 minutes/day)

1. Send 5–10 connection requests with personalized notes (Tier 2)
2. Follow up on any pending conversations
3. Message 3–5 warm contacts directly (Tier 1)
4. Check for profile viewers → send Tier 3 message if relevant

**Weekly goal:** 5 conversations started → 1–2 calls booked

---

## LINKEDIN PROFILE OPTIMIZATION (before you start)

Make sure your profile says what you do clearly:
- Headline: "Founder @ ArgusReach · AI-Powered Sales Development for Financial Services"
- About section: 3 sentences — what ArgusReach does, who it's for, what makes it different
- Featured: link to argusreach.com
- Banner: something professional / clean

People will check your profile before responding. Make sure it converts.
