## USAGE NOTES

- All emails go out signed as the client (PT practice owner, clinical director, etc.) — ArgusReach is invisible
- Physician outreach should feel collegial and peer-to-peer — not like a vendor pitch
- Mental health sequences are intentionally shorter — the space is sensitive, fewer touches
- Always include phone number in PT/MH sequences — physicians prefer to call back, not email
- Adjust for specific clinical specialty (ACL vs. spine vs. general PT) in the prospect data field
