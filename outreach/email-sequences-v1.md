## WRITING GUIDELINES FOR ALL SEQUENCES

1. **Always personalize Email 1** — reference the firm name, location, something specific if available
2. **Keep emails SHORT** — under 150 words for Email 1 and 2
3. **One ask per email** — always close with a single specific CTA (15-min call)
4. **No attachments on first contact** — never attach PDFs or decks on Email 1
5. **Plain text formatting** — no heavy HTML templates, no logos in first emails (lands in inbox better)
6. **Send from:** vito@argusreach.com (warmed domain)
7. **Sending window:** Tue–Thu, 8am–11am ET (highest open rates)
8. **Spacing:** Never send more than 1 email/day to same person; respect unsubscribes immediately
