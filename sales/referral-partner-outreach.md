# Referral Partner Outreach
## CPAs, Estate Attorneys, Business Brokers → Refer their RIA/professional services clients to ArgusReach

This is a multiplier. One CPA with 40 RIA clients could fill your pipeline faster than any cold outreach.

---

## THE STRATEGY

These professionals already have relationships with your ideal clients.
They don't compete with you — they complement you.
The pitch: "Send me your clients who need help growing. I'll handle it professionally. They'll thank you for the intro."

## TARGET PROFILES

**CPAs & Accounting Firms (Tampa Bay)**
- Best referrers for RIAs and insurance agents
- Their clients = business owners, HNW individuals, pre-retirees
- Apollo search: Title = "CPA", "Managing Partner", "Tax Partner" · Industry = Accounting · Employees 2–30

**Estate Attorneys**
- Work with HNW clients on wealth transfer — natural lead-in for RIAs
- Apollo: Title = "Attorney", "Partner", "Founding Partner" · Industry = Legal · Keywords: "estate planning", "trusts"

**Business Brokers**
- Their clients just sold a business = major liquidity event = perfect RIA prospect
- Apollo: Title = "Business Broker", "M&A Advisor", "Managing Director" · Industry = Financial Services

**Commercial Real Estate Brokers**
- Work with business owners constantly
- Natural fit for commercial insurance referrals

## THE REFERRAL ARRANGEMENT (keep it simple for now)

**Option A — Finder's fee**
$250 one-time for any referral that becomes a paying client.
Pay via Venmo/Zelle after client completes first month.

**Option B — Reciprocal referrals**
No money changes hands — you refer their clients to qualified RIAs/professionals in your network.

**What to say when they ask:**
> "If you refer a client to us and they sign on, we pay you $250 as a thank-you — one time, after their first month. Nothing complicated. And if there's ever a business owner who needs an introduction to [their service], I'll send them your way."

---

## FOLLOW-UP CADENCE

1. Send email (or LinkedIn DM after connecting)
2. If no reply in 5 days → one follow-up
3. If they respond positively → 15-min call → explain the service → propose finder's fee arrangement
4. After call → send a written summary with your Calendly and one-pager link
