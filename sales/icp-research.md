# ArgusReach — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Research

## Tampa Bay Market Overview

**Geographic focus:** Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon, Sarasota, Naples, Fort Lauderdale (expand after 5+ clients)

**Why Tampa Bay:**
- 4th fastest growing metro in the US (2020–2025)
- High concentration of financial services firms, insurance agencies, healthcare practices
- Business-friendly regulatory environment — high density of independent RIAs vs. wirehouses
- Strong referral culture among professionals — once you're in, doors open fast

---

## Vertical 1: Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs) & Wealth Management

### Who We Target
- **Firm size:** 2–15 advisors, $50M–$500M AUM
- **Titles:** Founder/Owner, Managing Partner, Lead Advisor
- **Pain:** Entirely referral-dependent; no proactive prospecting system
- **High signal events:**
  - Business owner had a liquidity event (sale, merger, PE recapitalization)
  - Executive receiving large equity comp (RSUs vesting, options, LTIP)
  - High-income earner approaching retirement (55–65)
  - Recent divorce (asset division)
  - Inherited wealth

### Prospect Search Criteria (Apollo.io)
```
Titles: "Financial Advisor", "Wealth Advisor", "Investment Advisor", "Managing Partner", "Founder"
Company Type: Financial Services
Employee Count: 2–50
Location: Tampa Bay, FL (50mi radius)
Industry: Financial Services, Investment Management
Exclude: Banks, broker-dealers, wirehouses
```

### Offer Framing for RIAs
- Pain: "You've built your practice on referrals — but referrals can't scale, and they dry up without warning."
- Solution: "We build a proactive pipeline of qualified prospects — business owners, executives, and HNW individuals — who are already in a moment of financial transition."
- Proof point: "B2B personalized cold email converts at 4–8% positive reply rate when targeting is precise and copy is relevant."

## Vertical 2: Independent Insurance Agencies

### Who We Target
- **Firm size:** 3–30 producers, commercial or mixed book
- **Titles:** Agency Owner, Principal, VP of Sales
- **Pain:** New client acquisition is expensive and unpredictable; producers spend too much time on prospecting vs. closing
- **High signal events:**
  - Business approaching renewal (6–8 weeks out)
  - Company headcount growth (hiring signal = likely benefits review)
  - New business formation / startup
  - Business acquisition (new owner needs coverage review)

### Prospect Search Criteria
```
Titles: "Agency Owner", "Principal", "VP Sales", "President"
Industry: Insurance
Employee Count: 3–75
Location: Tampa Bay + Sarasota + Naples
Book type: P&C + Group Benefits preferred
```

### Offer Framing for Insurance
- Pain: "Your best producers spend 60% of their time prospecting. That's not sustainable."
- Solution: "We run targeted outreach to your ideal commercial prospects — delivered to their inbox before your competitors get there."

## Vertical 3: Physical Therapy Practices

### Who We Target
- **Firm size:** 2–10 locations; owner-operated or small group
- **Titles:** Practice Owner, Clinic Director, Operations Director
- **Referral sources we target on their behalf:**
  - Orthopedic surgeons (post-surgical PT referrals)
  - Primary care physicians
  - Sports medicine doctors
  - Employers / HR Directors (corporate wellness, occupational health)

### Prospect Search Criteria (for their referral outreach)
```
Target: Orthopedic Surgeons, Sports Med MDs, PCPs, HR Directors at companies 100+ employees
Location: Within 10mi of PT clinic locations
```

### Offer Framing for PT Practices
- Pain: "You're fully dependent on physician referrals, but most surgeons have 5+ PT practices competing for their attention — and none of them are doing consistent outreach."
- Solution: "We run systematic, professional outreach to orthopedic surgeons and HR directors in your area, on your behalf."

## Vertical 4: Mental Health Practices

### Who We Target
- **Firm size:** Group practices, 3–15 therapists
- **Titles:** Practice Owner, Clinical Director, Operations Manager
- **Referral/channel sources we target:**
  - HR Directors at companies 50+ employees (EAP supplemental)
  - Employee Benefits brokers
  - Primary care physicians
  - OB/GYNs (postpartum referrals)

### Offer Framing
- Pain: "Caseload is inconsistent; word of mouth isn't enough to fill every therapist's schedule."
- Solution: "We build a pipeline of HR and EAP connections who can send you consistent referrals month over month."
