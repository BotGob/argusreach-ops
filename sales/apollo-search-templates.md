# ArgusReach — Apollo.io Search Templates

Copy-paste these directly into Apollo.io People Search when you sign up.
Each search is pre-built for a specific vertical and geography.

---

## HOW TO USE APOLLO

1. Sign in → People → Search
2. Apply filters below in order
3. Review first 20 results manually — do they look right?
4. Export (free plan: 50/month; paid: unlimited)
5. Download CSV → clean in Google Sheets → import to Airtable

## VERTICAL 1: RIA / Wealth Management (Tampa Bay)

**Filters:**
```
Job Titles (include ALL of these):
- "Financial Advisor"
- "Wealth Advisor"
- "Investment Advisor"
- "Managing Partner"
- "Founder"
- "Owner"
- "Principal"
- "Chief Investment Officer"

Company Industry: Financial Services, Investment Management

Company Employees: 2–50

Location: Tampa, FL + St. Petersburg, FL + Clearwater, FL + Sarasota, FL
(Set radius: 50 miles from Tampa, FL)

Exclude:
- Company name contains: "Bank", "Wells Fargo", "Merrill", "Morgan Stanley", "Edward Jones", "Ameriprise"
  (These are wirehouses — target independents only)

Keywords (company): "independent", "RIA", "registered investment", "wealth management", "advisory"
```

**Expected results:** 200–400 contacts in Tampa Bay

**Quality check:** Does the person have a LinkedIn? Are they at a firm with 2–20 employees? Is the firm name something like "[LastName] Wealth" or "[City] Advisory"? If yes, good fit.

## TIPS FOR BETTER RESULTS

- **Always review the first 20 manually** before exporting — Apollo quality varies
- **Check LinkedIn profile exists** — if no LinkedIn, email is more likely wrong
- **Filter by "Email Status: Verified"** if available on your plan
- **Export in batches of 50** for quality control — don't export 200 at once on first use
- **Add a custom field "Signal"** in the CSV for any notable trigger events you spot during review
- **Avoid exporting on Monday** — best to load campaigns Tue–Thu anyway
